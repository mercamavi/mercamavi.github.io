Cybersecurity is Everybody's Business

Improve your security posture with these easy tips:

  • Create Strong Passwords: More than 12 Characters,Uppercase,Lowercases,numbers and symbols.
  • Do not open e-mails from Strangers.
  • Do not click on links inside e-mails or open attachment from untrusted sources.
  • Set a secure password on your home Wi-Fi network.
  • Do not let your kids use your work computer.
  • Always use VPN to access to your company's network.

Advance one step with these Pro-tips:

  • Implement a Risk Management Strategy and mitigate risk from threats.
  • Put in place a Disaster Recovery Plan to minimize your system downtime .
  • Keep OS and firmware of your computers/devices up to date.
  • Monitor your system and conduct penetration testing.
  • Research about new trends,threats and vulnerabilities.
  • Develop cybersecurity employee training at all levels.
